City & Guilds: Qualified

City & Guilds is an accredited qualification. Hannah attended 'The College Of Animal Welfare' in Cambridge where she covered an variety of grooming related subjects as well as doing many practical assessments. She passed her City & Guilds Level 3 Dog Grooming with distinction.

ProTraining: Qualified Pet First Aider

Pets have accidents the same as humans and knowing what to do should the worst happen is essential for a dog groomer. Understanding basic first aid for your pet will give you peace of mind knowing we are able to assist them and prevent them from getting worse before professional help is given by your vet. Hannah has completed her pet first aid and continues to take refresher courses every 3 years.
